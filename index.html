RAILS GIRLS KENYA MEGACODING WORKSHOP
2020

Rails Girls comes to Nairobi Kenya for a Ruby on Rails workshop! We'll dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails.
2020.10.16 (FRI) ~ 17 (MON)

@ Riara University, Nairobi Kenya

Main Program

Lecture

Listen to Senior developers talk about basics of Ruby Language.

Sponsors

Sponsors talk about what they do and share opportunities with attendees.

Break-out Session

Attendees break out in groups and build a simple app.

Lightning Talks

Guest speaker share her journey into development.

Networking

Meet and Network with other developers + Group Photo.
