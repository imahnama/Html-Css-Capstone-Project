RAILS GIRLS KENYA MEGACODING WORKSHOP
2020

Hello world! Rails Girls comes to Nairobi Kenya for a Ruby on Rails workshop! We'll dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails. The event is brought to you by Rails Girls Nairobi, Rails Girls Mombasa and Rails Girls Kampala as part of Nairuby Conference Kenya

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Most of the speeches are given in english, but small group work is done in swahili. Please bring your laptop.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum.

2010-2020, Rails Girls. Contact us or follow us on Twitter and Facebook
